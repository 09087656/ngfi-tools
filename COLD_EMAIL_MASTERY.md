## ✍️ Email Body - The Formula

### Ideal Email Structure (75-100 words)

```
OPENING (2-3 words)
↓
PERSONAL DETAIL (show you researched them)
↓
PAIN POINT (agitate - make them feel it)
↓
SOLUTION (3 lines max)
↓
VALUE (specific - "10h+" not "productivity")
↓
CTA (1st person - "Can I show you?")
↓
SIGNATURE
```

### Trigger Words That Work

**Pain Trigger Words:**
- "waste"
- "frustrated"
- "tired of"
- "overwhelmed"
- "stuck"
- "missing out"

**Benefit Trigger Words:**
- "gain"
- "automate"
- "save"
- "boost"
- "finally"
- "without"

**Urgency Words:**
- "quick"
- "15 min"
- "this week"
- "this month"

## 🧪 A/B Testing Strategy

### What to Test (Priority Order)

**Priority 1: Subject Lines**
- Test 3-5 variants per segment
- Send to 100-200 people each
- Measure: Open rate improvement (5-10% lift possible)

**Priority 2: Email Length**
- Short (50 words) vs Medium (75 words) vs Long (125 words)
- Best: 75-100 words

**Priority 3: CTA Type**
- "Can I show you?" vs "Interested?" vs "Worth a chat?"
- Personalized CTAs = 202% better

**Priority 4: Opening**
- Direct pain mention vs Proof vs Curiosity
- Measure: Reply rate (not just opens)

### Testing Framework

```
Week 1: Subject line variants (divide 100 into 5 groups)
  → Keep best 2
  
Week 2: CTA variants (divide 100 into 3 groups)
  → Keep best 1
  
Week 3: Opening hook variants
  → Finalize
  
Week 4+: Scale with winning combo
```
