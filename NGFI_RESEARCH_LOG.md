## Red Flags & Filters Applied

### Excluded Prospects (Why?)
- Agency owners (not freelancers)
- Full-time employees (even if "freelance" in bio)
- No portfolio/proof of work
- Non-responsive to initial scoring
- Located outside francophone zones (unless EU-based)

### Included Criteria
✅ Freelancer status verified
✅ Active online (recent LinkedIn update or Malt profile)
✅ Relevant skill set (tech, creative, marketing)
✅ Located in francophone region (France, Belgium, Switzerland, Canada, Africa)
✅ 3+ years experience (entry-level okay if high potential)
✅ Professional presence (LinkedIn, website, or Malt)
