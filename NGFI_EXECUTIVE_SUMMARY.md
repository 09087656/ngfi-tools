## Campaign Strategy Summary

### Warm-Up Approach
✅ **NOT:** Blast 100 emails Day 1 (= spam folder)
✅ **YES:** Progressive volume (10 → 30 → 100 emails over 3 weeks)

### Segmentation
- Separate templates by sector (Developers ≠ Writers)
- Separate by tier (Tier 1 first, then Tier 2-3)

## FAQs

**Q: Why not send all 100 emails first week?**
A: Warm-up = better deliverability, learn what works, avoid spam folder

**Q: What if I get no replies in Week 1?**
A: Switch templates (to B or C), check email validity, pivot messaging

**Q: Should I chase everyone who doesn't reply?**
A: No — 1-2 follow-ups only (Day 5, Day 10). Then move on.

**Q: How do I know if an email is working?**
A: Open rate >25%, Reply rate >5%, Demo booking rate >2%

**Q: Can I send more than 100/day?**
A: No — Gmail will throttle. Stick to 15-25/day max for safety.

**Q: What if someone replies "not interested"?**
A: Thank them, don't follow-up. Move on to next prospect.

**Q: How do I track if emails were opened?**
A: Gmail + (external tracking = MailerLite, HubSpot, or manual log)

**Q: When do I stop sending and review?**
A: After each week (Weeks 1, 2, 3). Analyze → Adjust → Continue.
