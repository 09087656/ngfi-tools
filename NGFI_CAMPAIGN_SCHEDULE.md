## FOLLOW-UP STRATEGY

### Timing Rules
- **First follow-up:** 5 days after initial email
- **Second follow-up:** 10 days after initial email
- **Third touch:** Reserved for warm leads only

### Follow-Up Allocation
- **Week 2:** Follow-up on Week 1 emails (5-day rule)
- **Week 3:** Follow-up on Week 2 emails (5-day rule)
- **Week 4+:** Follow-up on Week 3 emails + 2nd touches

### Follow-Up Email Logic
1. **No open → Light touch** ("Maybe you missed this?")
2. **Opened but no reply → Softer CTA** ("Any questions?")
3. **Replied no → Don't follow-up** (respect the no)
4. **Opened + silent → One more try** ("Last chance")

---

## WARM-UP RULES (CRITICAL)

✅ **DO THIS:**
- Space emails 30 min - 2 hours apart (not blast all at once)
- Vary send times (9 AM, 10 AM, 10:30 AM, etc.)
- Personalize EVERY email (use their name, reference their sector)
- Track response rate daily
- Pause if bounce rate >5%
- Optimize templates based on Week 1-2 data

❌ **DON'T DO THIS:**
- Send all emails at same time
- Send same template to everyone
- Send more than 25/day to same domain (avoid spam filters)
- Ignore bounces (remove invalid emails)
- Blast everyone on Day 1
- Use generic subject lines

---

## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### IF Response Rate is LOW (<3%) in Week 1
- **Action:** Pivot to Template B (Pain-Point) - more storytelling
- **Adjust:** Longer subject lines, shorter body
- **Check:** Email validation - are they real addresses?

### IF Bounce Rate is HIGH (>5%)
- **Action:** Validate email list immediately
- **Remove:** All bounced addresses from database
- **Notify:** Idir of potential data quality issues

### IF No Replies by Wednesday Week 1
- **Action:** Switch to Template D (Teaser) - more curiosity
- **Send:** To designers + creative roles first
- **Test:** If they respond better, use for Week 2

### IF Replies are Negative ("Not interested")
- **Action:** Don't follow-up that person
- **Analyze:** What was the objection?
- **Pivot:** Adjust messaging for next cohort

## HANDOFF CRITERIA

When a prospect replies positively ("interested", "tell me more", "demo?"):

1. **Qualify:** Ask discovery questions
   - Current pain points?
   - How many hours/week on admin?
   - Budget awareness?
   - Timeline?

2. **Schedule:** Offer demo slot
   - "I have 15 min Tuesday 2 PM or Wednesday 10 AM"
   - Keep it short (15-20 min demo)

3. **Track:** Update database status
