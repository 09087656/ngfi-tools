### Mission

1. **Cold Email:** Warm-up outreach to freelancers (Week 1: soft test → scale)
2. **LinkedIn Tools:** Daily micro-tools + lead magnet (10 tools in 10 days)

### Target ICP
- French freelancers (Dev, Design, Writing, Marketing, etc.)
- 1+ years experience
- 2000€+ monthly revenue
- Frustrated with admin/time management
- Pain: Manual invoicing, tax calculation, time tracking

- Calls: 5-11 booked first month (conservative to optimistic)
