## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Personalize when you can** - Mention their specific work/sector
2. **Keep it short** - 3-4 short paragraphs max
3. **Mobile-friendly** - Test on phone
4. **Clear CTA** - One action (reply, click link, calendar)
5. **Social proof** - "We're testing with X freelancers already"
6. **Timing** - Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am (peak email open time)
